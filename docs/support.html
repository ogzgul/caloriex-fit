Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a meal?

Tap the + button next to any meal section (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks) on the main dashboard. Search for a food item by name and select your portion size.

How is my calorie goal calculated?

Your goal is calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula based on your age, height, weight, gender, activity level, and weight goal. You can update these values anytime in the Profile tab.

How do I edit or delete a food entry?

Swipe left on any food entry in the meal list to reveal the delete option. Tap the entry to edit the portion size.

Why isn't a food item showing up?

Try searching with different keywords. The database includes both Turkish and English food names. If a food item is missing, please contact us and we'll add it in a future update.

How do I reset my data?

Go to Profile → Reset App. This will permanently delete all your logs and profile data. This action cannot be undone.

How do I set meal reminders?

Go to Profile → Meal Schedule & Reminders. You can configure your meal times and enable/disable notifications for each meal.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Caloriex Fit works fully offline. All data is stored on your device.
