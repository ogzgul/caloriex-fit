Privacy Policy

Caloriex Fit – Calorie & Macro Tracker

Overview

Caloriex Fit is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information you enter — your profile, food logs, and water intake — is stored exclusively on your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account or registration required
  • No personal information sent to any server
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No third-party advertising
  • No location data

Data Stored On Your Device

The following data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework and never leaves your device:

  • User profile (name, age, height, weight, activity level)
  • Daily food and calorie logs
  • Water intake records
  • Meal schedule and notification preferences

Camera Permission

Caloriex Fit may request camera access for barcode scanning to identify food products. Camera usage is optional and only activated when you choose to scan a barcode. No images are saved or transmitted.

Notifications

The app may send local notifications for meal reminders and water intake reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on-device. No notification data is sent to any external server.

App Store & Apple

When you download the app through the App Store, Apple may collect certain data as described in Apple's Privacy Policy. This is outside our control and not related to how Caloriex Fit handles your data.

Children's Privacy

Caloriex Fit does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not contain any features directed at children.
